About Us

WHO WE ARE?


Welcome to the world of precision and excellence in sports equipment.
We are a pioneering Tennis Ball Manufacturing Company, headquartered in the
heart of the Sports Capital of India, Meerut. Since our inception in 1999,
Mark Industries has been committed to producing top-notch cricket tennis
balls, setting the industry standard for quality, innovation, and performance.

  OUR FOUNDER


Our journey began with the vision of Mr. Pankaj Khanna, a visionary
entrepreneur and sports enthusiast. His passion for cricket, coupled with an
unwavering commitment to quality, laid the foundation for Mark Industries.
Under his leadership, we have grown to become a trusted name in the sports
industry.

STATE OF THE ART MANUFACTURING


In our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, we have harnessed the most
advanced technologies and industry-leading expertise to produce cricket
tennis balls that consistently exceed the highest standards. Our commitment
to excellence is reflected in every step of our production process.


Our facility is equipped with cutting-edge machinery and technology, enabling
us to create cricket tennis balls that are not only of the highest quality but
also set the industry benchmark. We understand the importance of precision
in the game of cricket, and we take it seriously.


Quality control is at the heart of our operations. Our stringent quality control
processes are meticulously designed to ensure that every cricket tennis ball
bearing our name meets and surpasses the expectations of players, coaches,
and cricket enthusiasts alike. We leave no room for compromise when it
comes to delivering top-notch products.

OUR MISSION


Empowering Athletes : We are dedicated to empowering athletes with superior quality cricket tennis balls that enhance their performance and enjoyment of the game. We believe in making a meaningful impact on the sporting world by providing the tools for success.


Innovation and Excellence : We are committed to pushing the boundaries of innovation to constantly improve our products. Our mission is to excel in every aspect of tennis ball manufacturing, setting new industry benchmarks along the way.


Customer-Centric Approach: We prioritize our customers’ needs and preferences, striving to provide exceptional service and products that consistently exceed their expectations.


  OUR VISION


Global Reach: We aim to extend our reach to cricket enthusiasts around the world, becoming a leading supplier of high-quality tennis balls for the sport.


Sustainable Practices: We envision integrating sustainable practices into our manufacturing processes to minimize our environmental footprint and promote a greener future for sports.


Community Engagement: We aspire to actively engage with local communities, supporting the growth of sports at the grassroots level. Our vision includes fostering a love for cricket and promoting its values among the youth.
